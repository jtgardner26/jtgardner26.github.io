# A Dynamic Model of Socio-Political Positioning

*with Aseem Kaul*

## Abstract

We develop a dynamic model of how firms adjust their socio-political positioning over time in response to shifting political landscapes. The model captures the tension between commitment to existing positions and the strategic value of repositioning as political contexts evolve.
