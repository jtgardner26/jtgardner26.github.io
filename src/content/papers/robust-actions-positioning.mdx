# Robust Actions in Socio-Political Positioning

*with Haram Seo and Alessandro Piazza*

## Abstract

This paper develops a theory of robust action in the context of corporate socio-political positioning. We explore how firms navigate contested political landscapes by adopting positioning strategies that maintain flexibility across multiple audiences with divergent political preferences.
